Import Course History

...from your Quest transcript! This will let you see and share your course history with friends.
It's simple — just follow the easy steps below:

Step 1

Log in to Quest > My Academics > Unofficial Transcript

Step 2

After your transcript loads, select the entire page (Ctrl+A) and copy it (Ctrl+C)

Step 3

Paste (Ctrl+V) your transcript into the textbox to the right and let the magic happen!

We value your privacy

Flow uses your transcript to import your course history and share it with friends. We do not store grades or any other private information. See our privacy policy for more details.
